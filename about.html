About Us

Pottsy's Driving School.

Mr Jared Potter

At Pottsy's driving school, we have a reputation for safe, professional driving lessons at affordable prices.

‍Hi, I'm Jared Potter, a local Qualified driving instructor based in Woody Point, QLD, and serving the areas around it including the Redcliffe, Caboolture, Pine Rivers, and Northern Brisbane areas under my business Pottsy's Driving School. I have over 10 years of experience in instructing and supervising learner drivers in Queensland, am Blue Card certified to work with children, and consider myself to be a driving enthusiast.

‍
Pottsy's Driving School has a reputation for safe and professional driving lessons at realistic prices. Our aim is to make lessons as convenient as possible, and we take bookings that fit around your schedule. We offer high-quality personally tailored one-on-one instruction with reliable, qualified driving instructors with extensive knowledge of the QLD road rules, as well as knowledge of the routes commonly taken by QLD Transport testers. These routes commonly have difficult intersections or other failing points that cost learners their driving test. To date, Pottsy's Driving School is proud to have helped over 120 learners get their P's.

‍My students driving safely on the road and passing their probationary driving test are my highest goals!
